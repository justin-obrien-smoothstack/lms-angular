Library Management System

We created this Library Management System to sharpen and enhance our web development skills as part of the Smoothstack training.

LMS

Admin

The admin has access to perform CRUD operations on all of our data entities and override loans for borrowers.

LMS

Borrower

The borrower is able to look through available books and check them out. He can also see his current loans and return books.

LMS

Librarian

The librarian can edit the number of books at a branch and update the details of their branch such as the name, address, and phone number.
